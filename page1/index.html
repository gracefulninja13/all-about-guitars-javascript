eletric guitar

here are some facts about eletric guitars

    1 some people think you cant play a eletric guitar without an amplifier thats not always the case, you can play a eletric guitar without an amplifier its just alot quieter.
    2 Electric guitars can have a hollow, semi-hollow and solid body. They do not rely on chamber acoustic to produce tones, and the presence of hollow body can cause feedback and unintentional vibrations of the strings.
    3 First electric guitars were introduced in the 1930s. They used an amplifier to manipulate the shape of guitar tones.
    4 Electric guitars had one of the most profound impacts on the music history of modern world. It enabled the creation of many new genres and changed the look and sound of many old genres.
